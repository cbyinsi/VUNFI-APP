Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following categories of information:

1) Information You Provide

  • Account information (e.g., display name, household name, email address if you sign in or contact us).
  • Profile & household data (e.g., family members, roles, avatars or nicknames).
  • Chore & schedule data (e.g., task titles, assignments, recurrence rules, due dates, time logs, notes, attachments you choose to upload).
  • Support communications (e.g., questions, feedback, and any contact details you provide).

2) Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data (e.g., screens viewed, features used, in‑app events, timestamps).
  • Device & diagnostics (e.g., device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, performance data).
  • Approximate location if enabled by your device settings (used for language, region format, or time‑zone features; we do not collect precise GPS location).

3) Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication providers (if you choose a third‑party sign‑in method).
  • Analytics or error‑reporting services that help us understand performance and improve stability.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information (such as health, biometric, or precise location data).

How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and personalize the App (e.g., chore assignments, rotations, timers, reminders).
  • Sync your data across devices if you are signed in.
  • Maintain safety and integrity, detect and fix bugs, and prevent misuse.
  • Analyze usage to improve features and user experience.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support responses.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

How We Share Information

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting). They are bound by contractual obligations to protect your information and use it only as instructed.
  • Household members you invite may see shared household data such as tasks, schedules, and time logs.
  • Legal compliance when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell your personal information. We also do not share personal information with third parties for their own direct marketing without your consent.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Where EU/UK data protection laws apply, we process personal data under these legal bases:

  • Contract: To provide the App and related services you request.
  • Legitimate interests: To maintain and improve the App, ensure security, and prevent misuse.
  • Consent: For optional features (e.g., notifications) where required; you can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the App, fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, and comply with legal obligations. If you delete your account or request deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period unless we are required to retain it.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Sources: You, your devices, and service providers.
  • Business purposes: As described in How We Use Information.
  • Sharing: With service providers and household members as described above; no sale of personal information.
  • Rights: Know/access, delete, correct, limit use of sensitive personal information (not collected), and opt‑out of certain sharing. To submit a request, email awacleanifyapp@gmail.com.
  • Non‑discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided us personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to protect your information when transferring it internationally.

App Permissions

Depending on your settings and device, the App may request optional permissions, for example:

  • Notifications: To send reminders for chores and schedules.
  • Storage/Photos: To let you attach images or files to tasks (optional and user‑initiated).
  • Approximate location: To adapt locale and time‑zone features (disabled by default; not used for tracking).

You can manage permissions anytime in your device settings. The App remains usable with limited functionality if you deny optional permissions.

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” (DNT) signal. Because there is no industry standard for responding to DNT, we do not respond to these signals. We continue to review developments and may update this Policy if standards emerge.
